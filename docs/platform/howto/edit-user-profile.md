title: Edit your user profile
---

import {ConsoleIcon} from "@site/src/components/ConsoleIcons"

You can edit your name, job title, location, or other personal details in the Aiven Console.

1.  Click <ConsoleIcon name="user"/> **User information** in the top right.
1.  Select <ConsoleIcon name="user"/> **User profile**.
1.  Click **Edit**.
1.  Edit your profile and click **Save changes**.
