You can change the cloud provider or region of an Aiven App.

1. In your project, click <ConsoleLabel name="applications"/>.
1. Open your app.
1. In the **Cloud and network** section, click **Change cloud**.
1. Select a cloud provider and region.
1. Optional: Select a different plan.
1. Click **Change**.

The app is rebuilt in the new cloud provider and region.
This process may take a few moments.
