:::warning[End of life]

Aiven for Apache Cassandra® is approaching its end of life. You can create new services
until **December 3, 2025**. Existing services will continue to run until
**January 7, 2026**, at which point they will be deleted. To avoid data loss and service
disruptions, migrate your data out of Aiven for Apache Cassandra® before January 7, 2026.

[**Learn more**](/docs/platform/reference/end-of-life#aiven-for-apache-cassandra)

:::
